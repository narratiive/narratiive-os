# Campaign World Schema v1

## Purpose

The Campaign World translates the Growth Blueprint into a tangible creative system.

It defines how the strategy should look, feel, sound and behave in market.

The schema is designed to support:

- Notion
- Google Drive
- Canva
- Sora
- Replit Client Portal
- Future automation workflows

All Campaign Worlds must follow this structure.

---

# 1. Client Information

- `client_name`
- `website`
- `industry`
- `category`
- `growth_blueprint_link`
- `date_created`
- `campaign_world_version`

# 2. Strategic Foundation

## Core Problem

The primary growth challenge identified within the Growth Blueprint.

## Growth Opportunity

The commercial opportunity available to the brand.

## Strategic Positioning

The positioning statement established within the Blueprint.

## Audience Summary

Summary of:

- audience
- motivations
- barriers
- category behaviours

## Core Narrative

The narrative platform that underpins growth.

---

# 3. Creative North Star

## North Star Statement

Single sentence.

The idea all creative activity must reinforce.

Example format:

> “Serious coffee. Zero snobbery.”

## Strategic Role

What this idea is designed to achieve commercially.

## Emotional Job

How the audience should feel.

## Behavioural Change Required

The change in behaviour required to unlock growth.
